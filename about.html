About SneakSpray

Who are we?

Founded in 2023, SneakSpray aims to create nothing less than the highest quality footwear with a twist: these shoes never need to come off! Initially a passion project of 3 kids in a garage, we set out to not only provide comfort to those who despise traditional footwear, but to also create a fun and unique way for everyone to present themselves.



Our Mission

SneakSpray strives to create quality, alternative footwear for all ages. We vow to create shoes worthy of everything from the playground to the red carpet and will do what it takes to maintain both self-expression and confidence.
